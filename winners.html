Takeoff

In one of hockey's most famous pictures, Bobby Orr celebrates a Stanley Cup winning goal.

The Great one

Wayne Gretzky of the Edmonton Oilers lifts the Cup, celebrating one of four league championships in Edmonton.

The Other Bobby

A toothless Bobby Clarke celebrates a Cup win with the Philadelphia Flyers. This was in the days before the lift over the head became common.

First Kiss

The first kiss after winning it always goes to the Cup. It is considered bad luck to touch the Cup unless you win it.

Captain my Captain

The Captain of the winning team is first to touch the Cup.
